# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be an open, respectful, and technically rigorous space for research engineering collaboration.

Contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive
- critique ideas, not people
- explain trade-offs clearly
- welcome questions from people at different experience levels
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and exclusionary behavior

## Expected Behavior

- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Be explicit about assumptions and limitations.
- Keep discussions focused on reproducibility, safety, and engineering quality.
- Respect that some contributors may use this project in academic or industrial settings with stricter privacy requirements.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal insults or repeated hostile argument
- Publishing someone else's private data, credentials, or local environment details
- Deliberately misleading reproduction or benchmark claims

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors whose behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately and include relevant context.
